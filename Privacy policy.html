1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile information (if you sign in)

1.2 Device & Technical Information

  • Device model, operating system, and app version
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • Crash logs and performance data

1.3 Location Information

CeylonVisor may request access to your device location to enable AR experiences, map features, and nearby place discovery. Location access is optional and can be disabled in device settings.



1.4 Camera & Media

We may request access to your camera and gallery to enable AR features, image capture, and uploads. Media is only accessed when you grant permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Personalize your experience
  • Analyze app usage and improve features
  • Communicate with you

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., Firebase) for app functionality
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights, safety, or security

4. Third-Party Services

CeylonVisor uses third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, storage, analytics)
  • Google Maps

These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent

7. Children's Privacy

CeylonVisor is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
